Relational un-grouping (inverse of group)

SYNOPSIS

  alf ungroup [OPERAND] -- ATTR

DESCRIPTION

This operator flattens its operand by ungrouping the relation-valued 
attribute ATTR. 

EXAMPLE

  alf ungroup group -- supplying
